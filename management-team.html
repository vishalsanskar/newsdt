Management Team

Rhea Thahryamal

Chairperson

"Vision without action is a daydream. Action without vision is a nightmare."
As the Chairperson of Sanskar School, Rhea Tharyamal, a holder of a Master's Degree in Business Administration and Communication from Duquesne University in Pittsburgh, USA, embodies this philosophy. Sanskar School, under her leadership, envisions each student realizing their true potential, preparing them comprehensively for life's challenges. The institution thrives on endless possibilities, offering incessant opportunities for students to evolve into "Citizens of the World." Every day at Sanskar, the focus is on embracing diversity and fostering growth through ingenuity, reflection, and collaboration. The school ensures that students are not only academically nurtured but are also known, supported, healthy, happy, and fully prepared for success in the world beyond. Rhea Tharyamal's approach to education aligns with the belief that its purpose is to replace an empty mind with an open one. Sanskar School's educational philosophy advocates for well-formed minds rather than merely well-filled minds. The school's system of imparting education reflects a balanced blend of traditional methods and modern techniques, aiming to make school life a delightful experience. The motto, "Revival of the traditions," underscores the commitment to a holistic and enriching educational journey under Rhea Tharyamal's leadership.

Neelam Bhardwaj

Director - Academics

Mrs. Neelam Bhardwaj, Principal of Sanskar School, boasts over two decades of dedicated service, earning prestigious awards like the 'Achieving Excellence in Education' and 'Best Teacher Award' in 2015. Recognized for her leadership with the '6th Principals & Teachers Award' and 'DL Top Schools of India Award,' she has also been honored with the '12th World Education Award 2018' for sports education and the 'Edu Icon of Rajasthan 2022-23.' Notably, she received the 'Dronacharya Samman Award 2023.' Adding to her laurels, Mrs. Bhardwaj was recently honored with the Singhania Education Excellence Award in September 2023. Beyond awards, her commitment to holistic development is evident through the establishment of an Atal Tinkering Laboratory in 2018, promoting hands-on learning in electronics and robotics. Mrs. Bhardwaj's leadership extends to enhancing all facets of Sanskar School, emphasizing high-quality education, innovative teaching practices, faculty development, and state-of-the-art infrastructure. Her tenacity in instilling moral values shapes students into considerate citizens. As a visionary leader, Mrs. Bhardwaj values relationships, fosters trust, and creates win-win solutions by seeing both the individual and the bigger picture. Her approach revolves around holistic development and embracing new learning approaches while reviving traditions.

Gidhar Kumari

Director - Academics

Ms. Girdhar Kumari, the esteemed Principal overseeing the International Curriculum at Sanskar School, brings over two decades of profound experience in the realm of education. Her unwavering commitment has propelled Sanskar School to proudly hold affiliations with both the prestigious IB Curriculum and The Cambridge International Education. A distinguished alumna of Premier Convent School and College, Ms. Kumari infuses her leadership with a rich blend of experience and passion. Under her guidance, Sanskar School garnered recognition as an 'Outstanding School for Community Outreach in 2022,' a testament to her dedication to holistic education. Ms. Girdhar Kumari's visionary leadership goes beyond the confines of the institution, positioning her as a beacon of knowledge and inspiration in the educational landscape. Ms. Kumari is an active member of the IB Educator Networks and serves as a Programme Evaluation Leader for the IB. Her remarkable accolades include the Inspiring Educator Award and recognition as a Lion Club Facilitator, underscoring her significant contributions to the field of education. Through her visionary leadership, Ms. Girdhar Kumari continues to shape the future of education at Sanskar School, fostering a learning environment that truly reflects the best of both worlds.

Disha Tharyamal

Director - Operations & Student Engagement

Ms. Disha Tharyamal, Director of SDT Education Pvt. Ltd. is a young and dynamic entrepreneur. She is an alumnus of Mayo College Girls' School, Ajmer Rajasthan and holds a Bachelor's Degree in Marketing and Entrepreneurship from Drexel University, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. She is a dedicated health and wellness advocate who has positively influenced not only the holistic sports and wellness programs in Sanskar School at Jaipur but also the entire city of Jaipur by promoting healthy lifestyle, creative fitness regime and wellness programs for Jaipur citizens. She herself is a national-level squash player and works very closely with the Sports department of Sanskar School making the school as one with the best sports facility and holistic program for students of all ages. Her marketing and entrepreneurial skills has made Sanskar School Jaipur adopt many programs and initiatives making the school attracting admissions from every corner of the city beyond its capacity. Besides her academic and professional achievements, she is a fun loving and extremely compassionate person. She loves travelling and meeting new people. She strives to learn something new daily and also share the same with her family, friends, colleagues and the society in different ways.

Shiven Tharyamal

Director - Strategic Alliance & Finance

Mr. Shiven Tharyamal, Director of SDT Education Pvt. Ltd. is the youngest member of the family and the organisation yet with a vision of transforming the world with international standard education system and facilities from the foundation years till post graduation and beyond that will allow parents of all strata to fulfil their dreams for their children in their own cities. He has done his schooling from The Lawrence School, Sanawar, Himachal Pradesh and Pathways World School, Gurgaon Haryana. He is an alumnus of The Lee Strasberg Theatre & Film Institute, New York and School of Visual Arts, New York. He is a passionate film actor, script writer and director. His vibrant energy and commanding presence infuse the organization with enthusiasm, motivating young talents to strive towards their goals with focus and determination.
